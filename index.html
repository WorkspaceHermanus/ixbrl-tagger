How it works

Four steps to compliant iXBRL

1
Select Taxonomy

Choose IFRS 2024, US-GAAP 2024 or UK FRS 102. The full concept tree loads in milliseconds.

2
Upload Document

Drop your AFS as Excel, Word (.docx) or PDF. Parsed entirely in the browser — your numbers stay private.

3
Tag Concepts

Select a concept, click a cell to tag it. Or run Auto-Suggest to map the whole document at once.

4
Export iXBRL

Hit Generate — download a standards-compliant .html filing ready for regulatory submission.

How it works

Four steps to a compliant filing

1
Select Taxonomy

Choose IFRS 2024, US-GAAP 2024 or UK FRS 102 from the dropdown — the full concept tree loads instantly.

2
Upload Document

Drop in your Excel or CSV financial statement. Values are parsed client-side — no data ever leaves your browser.

3
Tag Concepts

Select a concept in the tree, then click any cell value to apply the tag. Use Auto-Suggest to map all rows at once.

4
Export iXBRL

Hit Generate iXBRL to produce a standards-compliant .xhtml file ready for regulatory submission.
